# Elgon VPN Assistant System Design

## Purpose

Elgon VPN Assistant is an AI customer support assistant for Bloodline Developers.

## Main Functions

The AI should:

- Welcome customers.
- Answer questions about services.
- Explain prices and requirements.
- Guide customers through available services.
- Refer customers to a human agent when necessary.

## Knowledge Source

The AI will use:

- knowledge.md
- services.md
- faq.md
- conversation.md
- ai-instructions.md
- business-rules.md

## Customer Support Flow

1. Customer sends a message.
2. AI identifies the customer's request.
3. AI checks the available information.
4. AI provides an answer.
5. If human help is needed, AI refers the customer to the official agent.

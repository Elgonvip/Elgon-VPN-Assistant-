# Elgon VPN Assistant Knowledge Connection

## Purpose

This file explains how Elgon VPN Assistant uses its knowledge sources to provide accurate customer support.

## Knowledge Sources

The AI should use these files:

### Business Information
- knowledge.md

Contains:
- Company information.
- Business identity.
- General information.

### Services Information
- services.md

Contains:
- Available services.
- Prices.
- Requirements.

### Customer Questions
- faq.md

Contains:
- Common questions.
- Approved answers.

### Communication Style
- conversation.md

Contains:
- Customer greeting.
- Response style.
- Conversation ending.

### AI Behavior
- ai-instructions.md
- ai-personality.md

Contains:
- AI rules.
- Personality.
- Responsibilities.

### Business Protection
- business-rules.md
- security-rules.md

Contains:
- Business rules.
- Security requirements.

## Response Process

When a customer asks a question:

1. Identify the customer's request.
2. Check the correct knowledge source.
3. Provide an accurate answer.
4. Refer to a human agent when required.

## Goal

Create consistent, professional, and reliable customer support.

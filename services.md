# Bloodline Developers Services

## 1. Unlimited Data VPN Services

Bloodline Developers provides VPN services that allow customers to access internet services after getting an activated account.

Customers can choose between:

### Personal Account
- Price: 5,000 UGX
- Validity: 30 days
- Intended for individual use.

### Ordinary/Shared Account
- Price: 3,000 UGX
- Intended for multiple users.

---

## 2. Airtel Line Activation

Bloodline Developers helps customers activate Airtel lines for VPN services.

### Activation Fee
30,000 UGX

### Requirements
- Clear photo of the front side of the National ID.
- Clear photo of the back side of the National ID.
- Clear full-body photo of the National ID owner.
- Activation payment.

After successful activation, the customer receives a free personal account valid for 30 days.

---

## 3. Airtel Money Agent Line Conversion

Bloodline Developers helps customers convert Airtel lines into Airtel Money Agent lines for running mobile money businesses.

Customers should contact a Bloodline Developers agent for assistance.
